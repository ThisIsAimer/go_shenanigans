Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services over the internet.
It provides on-demand access to resources like storage, servers, databases, and software.
With cloud computing, users can scale their infrastructure easily,pay only for what they use,and access their data from anywhere in the world.
It enhances collaboration, supports remote work, and reduces the need for costly physical hardware.
Cloud computing is the backbone of modern digital transformation, enabling innovation at unprecedented speed and scale.
